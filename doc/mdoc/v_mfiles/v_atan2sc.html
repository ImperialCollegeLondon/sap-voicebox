v_atan2sc

PURPOSE

V_ATAN2SC sin and cosine of atan(y/x) [S,C,R,T]=(Y,X)

SYNOPSIS

function [s,c,r,t]=v_atan2sc(y,x)

DESCRIPTION 

V_ATAN2SC    sin and cosine of atan(y/x) [S,C,R,T]=(Y,X)

 Outputs:
    s    sin(t) where tan(t) = y/x
    C    cos(t) where tan(t) = y/x
    r    sqrt(x^2 + y^2)
    t    arctan of y/x
