v_qrdotmult

PURPOSE

V_QRDOTMULT multiplies together two real quaternions arrays q=[q1,q2]

SYNOPSIS

function q=v_qrdotmult(q1,q2)

DESCRIPTION 

V_QRDOTMULT multiplies together two real quaternions arrays q=[q1,q2]

 Inputs:  q1(4n,...)
          q2(4n,...)   Two real quaternion arrays of equal size

 Outputs: q(4n,...)    The Hadamard product (i.e. .*) of the input arrays
